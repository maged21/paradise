About Us

Paradise Solutions was established in 1994 with the aim of marketing hardware and software in the field of information technology. The company's general strategy aimed to be a System Integrator, a provider of integrated solutions in the Egyptian market. Paradise Solutions achieved a good position in the market and was a major supplier to some of the most important clients such as Presidency, the Parliament, Paradise Solutions is Microsoft Independent Software Vendor ISV.
